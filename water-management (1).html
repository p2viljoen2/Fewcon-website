Water Management Services

Explore our comprehensive water management solutions designed to address your specific needs:

  • Water Use Licensing: Expert assistance in applications under Section 21 of the National Water Act (Act 36 of 1998), DWS risk matrix, authority engagement, and follow-up.
  • General Authorisation: Support for general authorisations related to water use.
  • Wetland Delineation and Functional Assessment: Accurate identification and functional evaluation of wetland areas.
  • Water Use Auditing and Management: Ensuring compliance and efficient water resource use.
  • IWWMP: Integrated Water and Waste Management Plans tailored to operations as required by DWS.
  • Water Balance Assessment: Detailed analysis to optimise water usage.
  • Hydropedology Assessments: Studying soil-water relationships for improved management.
  • GN704 Auditing: Compliance auditing with regulatory standards for mining operations.
