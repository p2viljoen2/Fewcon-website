Environmental Services

Discover our specialised environmental management solutions tailored to your needs:

  • Environmental Management Programme (EMPr): Comprehensive plans to minimise impacts and ensure compliance with standards.
  • Environmental Auditing (ECO services): Independent verification of compliance with approvals, licences, and EMPrs.
  • Maintenance Management Plans: Ongoing support for environmental upkeep.
  • Environmental MMP: Guidelines to prevent avoidable damage during maintenance, ensuring compliance with NEMA Section 24N.
  • Sec 34 Compliance Auditing: External auditing for regulatory compliance.
  • Contaminated Land Management: Site assessments to identify pathways, receptors, and risks, with reference to NEMA Section 30.
  • Waste Audit, Classification and Management Plans: Review and categorisation of waste streams in line with SANS 10234 standards.
